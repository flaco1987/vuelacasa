How It Works

As simple as calling mom.

Our system works for you — scanning, comparing, and alerting you when it's worth booking.

01
🔍

We Monitor

Our engine scans 32 Latin American routes from JFK every morning, comparing current prices against months of historical data to surface real opportunities.

02
📧

We Alert You

When a price drops 5% or more, or hits its historical low, you get an instant email with the route, price, airline, and exact travel dates.

03
✈️

You Book & Save

One click opens Google Flights with your route and dates pre-loaded. Pick the cheapest week and book — saving up to $300 per ticket.
